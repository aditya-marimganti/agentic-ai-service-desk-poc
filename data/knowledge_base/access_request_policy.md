# Access Request Policy

Access to internal systems, shared drives, and databases follows a role-based access control model. New access requests must be submitted through the Service Portal under "Access Requests" and require approval from your direct manager and, for sensitive systems, the relevant system owner.

Standard access requests (shared team folders, department drives, common internal tools) are usually granted within 1 business day after manager approval. Requests for sensitive systems (financial systems, HR records, customer data platforms) require additional approval from the data owner and may take 3-5 business days.

If you are changing teams or roles within the company, your old access permissions are not automatically removed. You must submit a new access request for your new role, and your manager should submit a separate request to revoke access that is no longer needed for your previous role.

All access grants are reviewed quarterly. You may be asked to confirm you still require access to certain systems; failure to respond within 10 business days may result in automatic revocation of that access as a security precaution.
