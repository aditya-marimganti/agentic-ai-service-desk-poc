# Password Reset Policy

All employee passwords must be reset every 90 days. You will receive an automated email reminder 7 days before your password expires. To reset your password, go to the self-service portal at password.internal.company.com and log in using your employee ID and current password.

If you have forgotten your password and cannot log in to the self-service portal, contact the IT helpdesk directly. You will need to verify your identity using your employee ID and a secondary form of verification, such as your registered personal email or manager's approval.

Passwords must be at least 12 characters long and include a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and at least one special character. Passwords cannot be reused from your last 5 previous passwords. Repeated failed login attempts (5 or more) will temporarily lock your account for 30 minutes as a security measure.
