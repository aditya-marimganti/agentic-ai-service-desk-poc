# New Hire IT Onboarding Checklist

On your first day, you will receive a company laptop and a temporary login for your employee account. You must change your temporary password to a permanent one within 24 hours, following the requirements outlined in the Password Reset Policy.

Your manager will submit initial access requests on your behalf for the systems your role requires, including email, shared drives, and any department-specific tools. This is separate from the standard Access Request Policy process, which applies to any additional access needed after your first week.

Within your first week, you should also install the VPN client (see VPN Setup Guide) if your role involves any remote work, and set up multi-factor authentication (MFA) on your account through the security portal. MFA setup is mandatory and your account will be restricted from accessing sensitive systems until it is completed.

If you do not receive your laptop or login credentials by your start date, or if any onboarding step is delayed, contact the IT helpdesk directly rather than waiting, since onboarding issues are treated as priority tickets.
