# VPN Setup and Troubleshooting Guide

To connect to the company VPN, download the GlobalProtect client from the internal software portal. Install the application, then log in using your standard employee credentials (same username and password as your email). Select "Company-HQ" as your connection gateway unless your manager has instructed otherwise for regional access.

If your VPN password has expired or you are unable to authenticate, this is often linked to your general account password. Resetting your main employee password (see the Password Reset Policy) will also resolve most VPN login failures, since both use the same credential system.

Common VPN issues include: slow connection speeds during peak hours (9-10 AM), which is expected and not a fault; being unable to access certain internal sites while connected, which usually means you need to be on the "Full-Access" gateway rather than "Split-Tunnel"; and repeated disconnections, which can often be fixed by switching from WiFi to a wired connection or restarting the GlobalProtect client.

VPN access is required for all remote work and must remain connected while accessing any internal company systems, including email, shared drives, and internal tools.
